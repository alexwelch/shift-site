as an extension of Primal’s mission to give back to the cycling community, Shift is an art auction celebrating the joys of bike culture. All proceeds will benefit BikeDenver.

Oct 26 2013 5-10 pm

Join Us

for an evening of
beer, food, bikes and art.

R.S.V.P.

@ReFuel 3459 Ringsby Court Denver, CO

This event is run in conjunction with the Mile High Urban CX Chaos race. The race will start and finish at ReFuel in November.

Brought to you by:

  • Primal:

    Since 1992, Primal has been advocating for cycling as a fun and satisfying way to stay in shape, keep our air clean, and enjoy the city from a close and personal perspective.

  • BikeDenver:

    Help Improve Denver for all bicyclists. Become a part of bicycle advocacy in Denver. Through your support and volunteer hours, we're making Denver a better place to ride a bike.

  • ReFuel:

    Located on the Taxi campus ReFuel is a spin-off from Fuel Cafe. A small menu of delicious sandwiches and a salad will fill your belly.

  • The Art Shoppe:

    We bring art to life, offering quality art supplies at wholesale prices.

  • Mile High Urban CX Chaos:

    Primal Wear’s Mile High Urban CX Chaos presented by Taxi is back for it’s 2nd year! We are moving across the Platte River to Mt. Taxi.
